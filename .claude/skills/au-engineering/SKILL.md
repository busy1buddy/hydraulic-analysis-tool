# Australian Water Engineering Reference

## WSAA Pressure Requirements
| Condition | Minimum Pressure | Standard |
|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Peak demand | 20m head (200 kPa) | WSAA Design Standard |
| Fire flow | 12m head (120 kPa) | WSAA / AS 2419.1 |
| Maximum static | 50m head (500 kPa) | WSAA Design Standard |
| Maximum transient | Pipe class rating | AS/NZS 2566 |

| Pipe Type | Max Velocity | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Distribution mains | 2.0 m/s | DN100-DN300 |
| Trunk mains | 1.5 m/s | DN300+ |
| Service connections | 2.5 m/s | DN20-DN50 |

## Joukowsky Equation
**dH = (a x dV) / g**
- dH = pressure rise (metres of head)
- a = wave speed (m/s)
- dV = velocity change (m/s)
- g = 9.81 m/s^2

## Key Australian Standards
- **WSAA** - Water Services Association of Australia Design Standards
- **AS 2280** - Ductile iron pipes and fittings
- **AS/NZS 1477** - PVC pipes and fittings
- **AS/NZS 4130** - PE pipes for pressure applications
- **AS 4058** - Precast concrete pipes
- **AS/NZS 2566** - Buried flexible pipelines
- **AS 2419.1** - Fire hydrant installations
- **AS 3600** - Concrete structures (for reinforced concrete tanks)
